Topology of Optical Processes

A fascinating and novel aspect of quantum mechanics is the interpretation of physical processes and systems dynamical evolution in terms of a geometric description (topology) of the quantum Hilbert states. In this broad field, my interest focuses on the topological properties of excitons and optical resonances.
